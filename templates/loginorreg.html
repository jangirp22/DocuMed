Purpose

DocuMed is a patient-doctor interface that offers a comprehensive solution for managing all medical document-related challenges. It provides a centralized repository to store a variety of patient documents, including medical certificates, scans, prescriptions, and lab reports, which patients can access at any time. Additionally, patients can securely share these documents with their doctors digitally while ensuring privacy.

Moreover, doctors can upload medical prescriptions and schedule appointments, enhancing the efficiency of medical record management and patient care.
